Home Cheese Pizza Chicken Biryani

This is an Odin recipe: layered, logical, and repeatable. Each component is built separately, then assembled with intention. No rushed shortcuts, no hidden steps—just a solid structure that always produces a rich, comforting lasagna with clean layers and deep flavor.

Ingredients (serves 4-6)

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef (or beef + chicken mix)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Cheese Layer:

  • 1½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Assembly:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook noodles

    2. Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until just tender. Drain and lay flat.

  2. Make meat sauce

    3. Heat olive oil in a pan. Cook onion until soft. Add garlic, then ground meat. Brown fully. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Simmer 15–20 minutes until thick.

  3. Prepare ricotta mix

    4. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, salt, and pepper until smooth.

  4. Assemble layers

    • Bottom: thin layer of meat sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Meat sauce
    • Mozzarella

    Repeat until finished. Top with mozzarella and parmesan.

  5. Bake

    6. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.

  6. Rest

    7. Let sit 10 minutes before slicing.
