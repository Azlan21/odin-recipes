This is an Odin-style recipe: minimal, structured, and repeatable. No shortcuts that hide the fundamentals, no unnecessary complexity—just core techniques you can reuse every time. Crisp on the outside, soft inside, and loaded with melted cheese, this pizza is designed to teach you why things work while still tasting great.

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Toppings:

  • ½ cup pizza sauce (or crushed tomatoes + pinch salt)
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: pinch dried oregano or basil

Instructions:

  1. Make dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar. Add warm water and oil. Mix and knead 5–7 min until smooth.
  2. Rest: Cover and let rise 30-45 min (until slightly puffy).
  3. Preheat: Oven to 475°F (245°C).
  4. Shape: Roll dough into a circle on a floured surface.
  5. Top: Spread sauce, add cheese, sprinkle herbs if using.
  6. Bake: 10-12 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  7. Serve: Cool 2 min, slice.
